# 🧵 Affordable Fashion Materials – E-commerce Website

A modern, minimal e-commerce website designed specifically for **fashion students** to purchase **affordable fashion design materials** for their projects, practice, and learning.

---

## ✨ Overview

This platform focuses on making fashion education more accessible by providing **budget-friendly, high-quality materials** in one place.

### 🎯 Target Audience
- Fashion design students  
- Beginner designers  
- Creative learners  

---

## 🛍️ Features

- 🧶 Fabric categories (Cotton, Muslin, Denim, Silk blends, etc.)
- ✂️ Sewing tools (Needles, Threads, Scissors, Measuring tapes)
- 📐 Pattern-making supplies (Pattern paper, Rulers, Tracing tools)
- 🎒 Beginner fashion kits  
- 💸 “Under ₹500” budget section  
- ❤️ Wishlist functionality  
- 🛒 Cart & smooth checkout experience  
- 📱 Fully responsive (mobile-friendly)

---

## 🎨 Design Style

- Minimal and clean UI  
- Black & White base with neutral tones  
- Modern typography  
- Editorial fashion-inspired layout  
- Focus on simplicity and usability  

---

## 📄 Pages

- **Home** – Hero section, featured products, categories  
- **Shop** – Product listings with filters  
- **Product Page** – Details, pricing, add-to-cart  
- **About Us** – Brand story and mission  
- **Contact** – Form and social links  
- **Cart & Checkout**

---

## 🧠 Concept

> “Design More, Spend Less.”

This project is built to support students who often struggle to find **affordable materials** while working on tight deadlines and budgets.
